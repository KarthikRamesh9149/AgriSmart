## Demo Script (2 minutes)

### Step 1: Open Mandya District
```
1. Click on search box
2. Type "Mandya"
3. Click "Mandya, Karnataka"
   → Map zooms to Mandya
   → Right panel opens with Digital Twin
```

### Step 2: Show Time Travel Slider
```
1. Look for "Time Travel" section
2. Slider shows 3 stops: 2000 | 2026 | 2050
3. Currently showing: 2026 data
   - Temperature: 44°C
   - Rainfall: 720mm
   - Heat Stress Days: 10
```

### Step 3: Drag Slider LEFT to 2000 (Baseline)
```
Say: "Let's see what Mandya's climate was in 2000..."

Drag slider all the way LEFT

Show values change to:
   - Temperature: 41.2°C  ← COOLER
   - Rainfall: 936mm      ← MORE RAIN
   - Heat Days: 0         ← NO HEAT STRESS
```

### Step 4: Drag Slider RIGHT to 2050 (Future)
```
Say: "Now let's project 50 years into the future..."

Drag slider all the way RIGHT

Show values change to:
   - Temperature: 47.5°C  ← HOTTER (+6.3°C)
   - Rainfall: 540mm      ← DRIER (-396mm)
   - Heat Days: 38        ← SEVERE HEAT STRESS
```

### Step 5: Explain the Impact
```
"Notice the stark difference:
- Temperature will rise 6.3 degrees
- Rainfall will drop by 40%
- Heat stress days will jump from 0 to 38 per year

This is why sustainable farming practices matter."
```

### Step 6: Show Other Tabs
```
Click "Crop Matchmaker" tab:
   "Based on this climate data, here are recommended crops..."

Click "Policy Simulator" tab:
   "We can compare government vs AI farming strategies..."
```
