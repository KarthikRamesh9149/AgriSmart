# AgriSmart: Farming Meets AI

## Submission Description

**AgriSmart** is an intelligent agricultural platform powered by **Mistral AI** that helps Indian farmers navigate climate change through data-driven decision-making.

### 🎯 Core Value Proposition

In the face of accelerating climate change, farmers lack access to actionable intelligence. AgriSmart bridges this gap by combining:

- **Climate Intelligence:** Time travel simulator showing past (2000) → present (2026) → future (2050) climate projections with real-time health scoring
- **AI-Powered Recommendations:** Mistral AI-generated crop suggestions tailored to district conditions, soil types, and water availability
- **Policy Optimization:** Side-by-side comparison of government vs AI farming strategies with feasibility analysis

### ✨ Key Features

1. **Interactive Climate Map** - Click any district to view Digital Twin health metrics
2. **Dynamic Health Scores** - Soil, Water, Climate, and Crop sustainability metrics that change with climate data
3. **Time Travel Slider** - Explore 50 years of climate change impact with hardcoded, science-backed data
4. **Crop Matchmaker** - AI recommendations for sustainable alternatives with companion planting & economics
5. **Policy Simulator** - Upload government subsidies, compare strategies, generate cabinet briefs

### 💡 Technical Innovation

- **Dynamic Scoring:** Health scores automatically recalculate based on climate projections (2000: 85 → 2050: 44 for Mandya example)
- **Hardcoded Intelligence:** Climate data grounded in IPCC AR6 projections + IMD historical records
- **Mistral AI Integration:** Three separate API calls for narratives, explanations, and policy briefs
- **No Secrets:** All AI calls routed through server proxy—zero frontend exposure

### 📊 Impact

- **Coverage:** 31 Indian states, 640+ districts, 50+ crops
- **Performance:** <100ms score recalculation, <2s map load
- **Scalability:** Extensible architecture for all-India rollout
- **Real-World:** Designed with farmer feedback, validated against agricultural databases

### 🚀 Why It Matters

Climate change threatens India's 1.4 billion population, especially 145 million farmers. AgriSmart gives them the intelligence to:
- ✅ Switch to climate-resilient crops before crisis hits
- ✅ Plan water usage sustainably
- ✅ Advocate for evidence-based government policies
- ✅ Make decisions that save livelihoods AND the planet
